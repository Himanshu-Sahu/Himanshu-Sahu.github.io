Crimes against women have more than doubled over the past ten years, according to latest.

As many as 2.24 million crimes against women were reported over the past decade: 26 crimes against women are reported every hour, or one complaint every two minutes

The semantic meaning of "crime against women" is direct or indirect physical or mental cruelty to women. Crimes directed specifically against women and in which only women are victims are characterised as "crimes against women".
